LEGENDARY CHRONOLOGY

Trace the rise, fall, and resurrection of the Ghost of the Uchiha

Warring Clans Era

A Childhood of War

Born the eldest of Tajima Uchiha's five children, Madara lost three siblings young, leaving only his brother Izuna at his side. His ferocious natural chakra let him cut down adult Senju before he had even awakened his Sharingan, earning him a reputation as a genius prodigy of the battlefield.

Riverside Rivalry

A Friendship Across Enemy Lines

In rare moments of peace, Madara befriended a boy his own age, skipping stones and dreaming of a world where children wouldn't have to fight. Only later did they learn the truth — Madara was an Uchiha, the boy was Hashirama Senju. Bound by clan duty, Madara severed the friendship, and the resolve awakened his Sharingan for the first time.

Era of Warring States

Founding of Konoha

After battling Hashirama Senju for years, Madara agreed to unite their clans. Together, they founded Konohagakure, the Village Hidden in the Leaves, establishing a template of peace for the shinobi world.

Valley of the End

The Grand Betrayal

Fearing the Uchiha would be marginalized, Madara left the village. He returned with the Nine-Tails under his control, clashing with Hashirama in a battle that carved the Valley of the End. Though defeated, Madara faked his death using Izanagi to plot his ultimate plan.

Decades in Hiding

The Rinnegan Awakens

Living in secret with a graft of Hashirama's flesh embedded in his own body, Madara waited out his final years. In old age, the transplanted cells finally activated, restoring his lost eye and awakening the legendary Rinnegan — power enough to summon the Demonic Statue of the Outer Path itself.

Third Shinobi World War

The Making of Obito

Finding a dying Obito Uchiha, Madara rebuilt him with Hashirama's cells and patiently corrupted him through orchestrated tragedy. With his dying breath, Madara passed on his knowledge of the Sage of the Six Paths, the Eye of the Moon Plan, and even his own name — leaving Black Zetsu behind to guide his successor.

4th Great Ninja War

Reanimation & Domination

Summoned via Edo Tensei, Madara confronted the entire Allied Shinobi Forces single-handedly. Demonstrating god-like power, he summoned meteors, activated Perfect Susanoo, and laid waste to the army, proving his legendary status.

Six Paths Awakening

Infinite Tsukuyomi

By becoming the Ten-Tails Jinchuriki, Madara attained the power of the Sage of Six Paths. He opened his third eye—the Rinne Sharingan—and reflected it off the moon, casting the Infinite Tsukuyomi to trap the world in a dream of eternal peace.

Final Moments

Breaking the Curse of Hatred

Betrayed by Black Zetsu's ancient machinations, Madara watched his life's plan collapse. In his last breaths he finally saw the flaw in his own philosophy, admitted the superiority of Hashirama's belief in peace, and reconciled with his oldest friend before passing on for good.

SHINOBI PROWESS

Examine the world-shattering techniques of the legendary Uchiha leader

Perfect Susanoo

Ultimate defense & offense

A colossal manifestation of blue chakra that takes the form of an armored samurai warrior. Its size matches mountains, and a single swing of its blade can cleave landscapes.

Shattered Heaven

Space-time Ninjutsu

Madara draws massive meteors down from the upper atmosphere using the Rinnegan. If the first meteor is stopped, he simply summons a second to crush all remaining resistance.

Limbo: Border Jail

Rinnegan Kekkei Genkai

Madara projects invisible clones into the phantom world of Limbo. These clones are completely undetectable to normal shinobi and can defend or strike with the same physical force as Madara himself.

Wood Style Sage Mode

Senjutsu Mastery

Having absorbed senjutsu chakra from Hashirama's own cells, Madara could synchronise it with his own and enter Sage Mode instantly — a feat that normally demands years of training — granting him heightened senses and monstrous physical power.

Izanagi

Reality-Altering Genjutsu

The ultimate technique of the Uchiha and Senju combined, Izanagi briefly rewrites reality to turn fiction into truth. Madara scheduled it to activate after his "death" at Hashirama's hands, trading his right eye's vision for a second life spent in hiding.

Unrivalled Taijutsu

Physical Combat

Even unarmed, Madara could calmly demolish thousands of soldiers from the Fourth Division with bare hands, disarming and countering attacks from every direction while overpowering opponents twice his size with terrifying ease.

MIND OF THE GHOST

The convictions, contradictions, and ultimate legacy of the Uchiha leader

Curse of Hatred

Personality

Once a kind, reasonable boy devoted to protecting his brother Izuna, Madara's heart hardened into bitterness after Izuna's death, embracing revenge over compromise — yet his bond with Hashirama left him never fully consumed by hatred until his final reconciliation.

Eye of the Moon Plan

Philosophy

Convinced the cycle of war was inescapable and humanity incapable of changing, Madara devoted his final decades to a plan that would force eternal peace upon the world through the Infinite Tsukuyomi — believing wholeheartedly that the ends justified any means.

Respect for Hashirama

Rivalry

No opponent ever earned the regard Madara held for Hashirama. Their decades-long stalemate left him with competing feelings of resentment and admiration, and he would gladly delay his own plans just to prolong a fight against his oldest rival.
